About

Taslima Begum


Graphic Designer and Front End Web Developer

I am a graphic designer living in New York and currently working in a non-profit organization. I moved to NY two years ago to use my skills in a creative, exciting environment. I have a masters in business management and diploma in Graphic design. You will see in my portfolio that I have experience doing illustration, graphic design and front-end development and have proudly worked with some fantastic clients and brands: Dell, Flugger and several Danish newspapers.

I have experience in all elements of print design but have been simultaneously finding a new place in my heart for web development. I am masterful in the Adobe Creative suite programs including InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator. Additionally, I am knowledgeable in HTML, CSS and JQuery.

To further my love, appreciation and knowledge of web programming, I am currently attending a 32-week certificate program in Web Development at the New York Code + Design Academy.
